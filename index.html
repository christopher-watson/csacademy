Requirements

In order to apply for the academy you must meet the following criteria:
  • Currently a Grade 8 or Grade 9 Student
  • C or higher average in Core Classes
  • Interest & Passion for working hard & learning!
In order to graduate from the academy you must maintain a 3.0 GPA in all Academy Courses.
